Fiorella Bourgeois is a Phd candidate in political sociology at the School of Advanced Studies in Social Sciences (EHESS) in Paris, France.

Thesis

She is conducting a thesis untitled "Hacking the State : Addressing the State in context of norms and legitimation processes reconfiguration in Taiwan. From a hacker network to the Executive Yuan".

Research topics

  • Sociology and history of free and open source software
  • Sociology of norms
  • Sociology of petition and complaint systems in Taiwan
  • Sociology of the State and contemporary administration in Taiwan
  • Sociology and history of e-government and deliberative democracy in Taiwan
